Home Delivery Network Limited purchased through its subsidiary company Parcelpoint Limited the domestic B2B and B2C businesses of DHL Express (UK) Limited. The new corporate identity for HDN and Parcelpoint will be Yodel. All our existing products and services are still available and will be working just as efficiently. Please continue to access your existing services in exactly the same way via the normal website and be reassured that your customers' track and trace service remains unchanged.
